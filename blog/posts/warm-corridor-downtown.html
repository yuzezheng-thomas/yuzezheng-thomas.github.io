STREET NOTES · CHICAGO · 21 MAY 2026

A Warm Corridor Through Downtown

Downtown Chicago can feel dense and loud, but the right light simplifies it. At sunset, the street becomes a corridor: vertical lines, warm reflections, and small movements passing through the frame.

I waited for the glow to sit between the buildings and used the traffic as a way to hold the bottom of the image. The result is less a record of one intersection than a note about rhythm in the city.
