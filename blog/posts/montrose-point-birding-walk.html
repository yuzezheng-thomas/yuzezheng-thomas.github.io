LOCATION GUIDE · MONTROSE POINT · 18 MAY 2026

Montrose Point As A Birding Walk

Montrose Point is one of the most reliable places in Chicago for bird photography. Morning is usually the best time: softer light, more activity, and fewer interruptions along the paths.

A longer focal length helps, but the more important tool is patience. I try to move slowly, watch where birds return, and choose backgrounds before raising the camera.
